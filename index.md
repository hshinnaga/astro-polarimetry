# Profile

I am an associate professor of the Astrophysics Group of Kagoshima University, located on the Kyushu island of Japan. 

Before joining the Astrophysics Group of Kagoshima University, I worked at the East Asian ALMA Regional Center (EA ARC), Nobeyama Radio Observatory, the Subaru Telescope, and at California Institute of Technology Submillimeter Observatory (CSO) as a staff research scientist. I was also an affiliated faculty of University of Hawaii at Hilo.

The ALMA antennas are located at Atacama Desert in Chile.
