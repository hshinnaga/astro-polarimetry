# Profile
I am an associate professor of the Astrophysics Group of Kagoshima University, located on the Kyushu island of Japan.  The ALMA antennas are located at Atacama Desert in Chile. Before joining the Astrophysics Group of Kagoshima University, I worked at the East Asian ALMA Regional Center (EA ARC), Nobeyama Radio Observatory, the Subaru Telescope, and at Califoria Institute of Technology Submillimeter Observatory (CSO) as a staff research scientist.  I was also an affiliated faculty of University of Hawaii at Hilo.
