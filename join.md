# Join Our Research Group

I am always looking for motivated graduate students who are interested in radio astronomy, astronomical polarimetry, and the wonders of the Milky Way.

## For Prospective Students
Kagoshima University provides a rich environment for astronomical research. If you are interested in joining my group, please consider the following:

* **Research Topics:** Star formation, magnetic fields, and interstellar medium.
* **Facilities:** Access to VERA and other radio telescopes.

## How to Apply
If you are interested in Master's or Ph.D. programs, please contact me via email with your CV and a brief description of your research interests.

[Contact Information is on the Home page](https://hshinnaga.github.io/astro-polarimetry/index.html)
