# Join Our Research Group

I am always looking for motivated graduate students who are interested in radio astronomy, astronomical polarimetry, and the researches of the Milky Way.

## For Prospective Students
Kagoshima University provides an interactive environment for astronomical research. If you are interested in joining my group, please consider the following:

* **Research Topics:** Star formation and evolution, interstellar medium surrounding the supermassive black hole of our Galaxy, and the roles of magnetic fields in their evolution.
* **Facilities:** Access to VERA, Iriki optical/infrared 1-m telescopes, Nobeyama 45m telescope, and other radio telescopes.

## How to Apply
If you are interested in Master's or Ph.D. programs, please contact me via email with your CV and a brief description of your research interests.
